Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MyPinkAssistant.

How do I look up customer information?

Just type their name in the chat — first name, last name, or both. MyPinkAssistant will find them even if you misspell it.

Can I look up customer birthdays?

Yes — just ask. Try "Who has birthdays this month?" or "Any birthdays this week?" The app returns a list sorted by how soon each birthday is, so you can reach out at exactly the right time. If you have a team or a unit, you can also ask "Who on my team has a birthday this month?"

How do I add a new customer?

Type it like you'd text it — something like "New customer Jane Smith, 555-123-4567, jane@email.com." The app will confirm the details before sending it to MyCustomers.

How do I enter a customer order?

Tell the chat what your customer wants in plain language — no SKU numbers needed. The app matches the products from the Mary Kay catalog and places the order in MyCustomers automatically after you confirm.

Do I need to manually enter my personal inventory orders into my inventory?

No — when you place a personal inventory order through marykayintouch.com, those items are added to your on hand stock automatically. No manual entry needed.

How does MyPinkAssistant keep my personal inventory organized?

When you enter a customer order, those items are automatically removed from your on hand stock. You can also update any item manually through chat, set par levels so MyPinkAssistant alerts you when something is running low, and print a PDF of your current inventory at any time.

How does the customer import work?

When you sign up and connect your InTouch account, your existing customers and order history are pulled in automatically. You can look up customer info or enter new orders right away.

Can I look up order history?

Yes — just ask. Try "What did Sarah order last time?" or "Show me Jane's last 3 orders." When you sign up, your existing order history from MyCustomers is imported automatically, so you can look up your customers' complete order history right away.

Does the app help me remember to follow up with customers?

Yes. Ask "Who should I follow up with?" and MyPinkAssistant will surface customers who are due for a check-in based on their order history. Tap the circle next to a name to pre-populate a follow-up text message you can send right from your phone.

Can I see business stats or reports?

Yes — just ask in plain English. Try "How many customers do I have?", "Who ordered last month?", "What's my total sales this year?", or "Who has ordered more than 3 times?" The app runs the numbers and answers right in chat — no spreadsheet required.

I'm a Mary Kay director. Can I track my unit?

Yes, and it's included at no extra cost. Ask things like "Show me my team list," "Who's working on Great Start?", "What's everyone's Star Consultant status?", or "Who's registered for Seminar?" Your unit data syncs automatically from InTouch every night so it's always current.

Is there a Spanish version?

Absolutely! You can switch the chat language to Spanish in Settings and the app will respond in Spanish for all your customer and order questions.

Is there a referral program?

Yes! Share your referral link — found in Settings — and new subscribers get their first month free. After their first paid month, you get a free month too. It stacks, so the more you share, the more you earn.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes, and you can make it an app! Open mypinkassistant.com in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android), tap the share or menu button, and choose "Add to Home Screen." It will appear on your home screen and open like an app — no download required. If you need help, just type "app" in chat for detailed instructions.

Is my InTouch password safe?

Yes. Your InTouch credentials are encrypted before being stored and are never readable in plain text — not even by us.

What do I need to sign up?

An active Mary Kay consultant account with InTouch credentials. That's it.

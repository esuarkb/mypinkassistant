What can I say in chat?

Just type it like you'd text it. Here are some examples to get you started.

Customers
  • "Look up Jane Smith"Pulls up her contact info, birthday, and recent orders
  • "Who has birthdays this month?"Also try "this week" or a specific month
  • "Who should I follow up with?"Surfaces customers due for a check-in based on order history. Click the pink button to send a follow-up text right from your phone.
  • "New customer Jane Smith, 555-123-4567, jane@email.com"The app confirms details before sending to MyCustomers
  • "Who are my customers in Huntsville?"Look up customers by city
  • "Who buys Timewise?"Find customers by product
Orders
  • "What did Sarah order last time?"
  • "Show me Jane's last 3 orders"
  • "Jane wants the Timewise Repair serum and the CC cream"Plain English — no SKU numbers needed. The app confirms before sending the order directly to MyCustomers.
Inventory
  • "How many Timewise sets do I have on hand?"
  • "Set [product] par to 3"
  • "Set my par level for [product] to 2"MyPinkAssistant will alert you when stock falls below your par level
Business Stats
  • "How many customers do I have?"
  • "Who ordered last month?"Also try "in April", "this quarter", or a date range
  • "What's my total sales this year?"
  • "Who has ordered more than 3 times?"
  • "Who has never ordered?" / "Who has ordered only once?"
Team & Unit
  • "Show me my team"
  • "Who is close to their next Great Start bundle?"
  • "What's everyone's Star Consultant status?"
  • "Who's registered for Seminar?"
  • "Any consultant birthdays this month?"
